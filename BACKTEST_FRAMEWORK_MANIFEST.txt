COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS
================================================================================

"Connection failed"
  Restart TradingView with --remote-debugging-port=9222

"No strategy results"
  Ensure strategy is loaded and visible on chart

"Script hangs"
  Increase wait times or test with fewer stocks

"No data"
  Verify sufficient historical data is available

"Permission denied"
  Run: chmod +x *.js
